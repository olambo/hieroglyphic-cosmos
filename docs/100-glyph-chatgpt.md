  # Hieroglyphic Cosmos: Top 100 Hieroglyphs (with Gardiner Codes)

**Project Reasoning and Selection Principles:**  
This curated list is intended for the **Hieroglyphic Cosmos** project, aimed at creating a museum-quality visual mapping of Egyptian hieroglyphs onto galactic coordinates. Selection criteria:  

1. **Astronomical relevance:** Prioritized glyphs representing stars, sun, moon, sky, and celestial phenomena.  
2. **Mythological importance:** Included major deities and sacred animals central to Egyptian cosmology.  
3. **Symbolic depth:** Life, death, eternity, time, and ritual symbols were emphasized for their cosmological significance.  
4. **Visual impact:** Chose visually distinctive and dynamic hieroglyphs suitable for large-scale artwork.  
5. **Scholarly clarity:** Gardiner codes included to ensure precise reference and integration into the digital mapping and visualization pipeline.  

The list balances **recognizability, symbolic richness, and aesthetic potential** for public display, academic engagement, and artistic storytelling.
