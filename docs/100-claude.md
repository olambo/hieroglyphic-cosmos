## Quality Control Keywords

### Avoid These (Common AI Art Problems)
- `detailed` (leads to over-complexity)
- `realistic` (not suitable for hieroglyphs)
- `3D` (wrong format for your needs)
- `colorful` (stick to B&W for consistency)
- `ornate` (contradicts minimal aesthetic)

### Emphasize These Instead
- `minimal`
- `clean`
- `geometric`
- `symbolic`
- `elegant`
- `balanced`
