# AI SVG Generation Cost Analysis for Hieroglyphic Cosmos

*Cost estimates for generating 300-800 hieroglyphic SVGs using AI tools*

## Budget-Friendly Options

### Recraft AI (Recommended)
- **Free tier**: Available with no credit card required
- **Paid plans**: Around $10-20/month for moderate usage
- **Vector generation**: 2 credits per SVG
- **Best for**: Starting with free tier, scaling to paid as needed

### ArtificialStudio
- **Cost structure**: 16 credits per SVG generation
- **Pricing**: 1,250 credits for $10 (approximately 78 SVGs)
- **Per SVG cost**: ~$0.13 per hieroglyph
- **Best for**: Predictable bulk generation

## Premium Options

### Adobe Illustrator with AI
- **Annual plan**: $22.99/month
- **Month-to-month**: $79.49/month
- **Commitment plan**: $52.99/month
- **Free trial**: 7 days (could generate large batches)
- **Features**: Text-to-vector with Firefly AI, full editing capabilities

## Cost Projections for Hieroglyphic Cosmos

### For 300 Hieroglyphs:
- **Budget route** (ArtificialStudio): ~$39 total
- **Mixed approach** (Recraft free + paid): $20-30 total
- **Premium sprint** (Adobe 7-day trial): $0 (if batched efficiently)

### For 800 Hieroglyphs:
- **Budget route** (ArtificialStudio): ~$104 total
- **Mixed approach** (Recraft free + paid): $40-60 total
- **Premium sprint** (Adobe 7-day trial): $0 (requires intensive workflow)

## Recommended Strategy

1. **Start with free tiers** to test quality and workflow
2. **Use Recraft AI** for bulk conversion of existing hieroglyph images
3. **Adobe trial** for complex/custom hieroglyphs that need text-to-vector generation
4. **ArtificialStudio** as backup for consistent pricing per SVG

## Bottom Line

**Total project cost: $40-100** for complete SVG generation using specialized AI tools.

This is significantly more cost-effective and consistent than:
- Hiring a designer ($1000s)
- Sourcing existing hieroglyphs (licensing issues, inconsistent styles)
- Using general-purpose LLMs (poor SVG quality)

The free tiers provide excellent opportunity to validate quality and workflow before committing to paid plans.
