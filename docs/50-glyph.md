### Hieroglyph Download List

**Family A: Man and his occupations**
* **A40**: "Osiris" (the god) - You'll need to look for this in the A family or C (anthropomorphic deities) family, as it's a deity. The link you provided, `39The A family (man)`, and `36The C family (gods)`, will likely contain this one. It's a common glyph.

**Family B: Woman and her occupations**
* **B1/E13**: "Bastet/Cat" - The Gardiner code for the cat is E13. The B family is for women, so B1 is a seated woman. You will likely find the cat (`E13`) in the E family, but you have the B1 code for "Bastet/Cat" which is likely a modern choice. You'll need to check the following links: `37The B family (woman)` and `44The E family (man)`.

**Family C: Anthropomorphic deities**
* **C20**: "Ptah" - This is in the C family. Go to `36The C family (gods)`.
