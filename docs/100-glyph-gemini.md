This is a curated list of 100 significant Egyptian hieroglyphs selected for the "Hieroglyphic Cosmos" artistic project. The selection criteria prioritize cultural and historical importance, visual distinction, and cosmic or thematic resonance suitable for a museum-quality artwork. The list is designed to be both scholarly and artistically useful, blending well-known symbols with lesser-known but symbolically rich glyphs. The hieroglyphs are organized into logical sections to facilitate thematic mapping for the final artistic rendering.
